\chapter{Selection Binning}\label{app:bintemplates}

Runs 2-8 data and MC were used to tune the algorithm for producing the non-uniform rectangular sample binning. The distribution of events binned using the original uniform binning is shown for all samples in Figure \ref{fig:th2dbinapp}. The distribution of events binned using the final non-uniform rectangular binning is shown for all samples in Figure \ref{fig:th2polybinapp}. For the non-uniform rectangular binning, the bins containing the largest amount of events are in the peak regions at high angle and $\sim$500 MeV.

Figure \ref{fig:th2polybinreset} shows the full distributions out to 30 GeV, for each sample.
